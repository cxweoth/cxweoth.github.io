Welcome to my personal website! I am **Tsung-Hsuan Hung (TH)**, a doctoral student based in Onna, Okinawa, Japan. 

My research primarily focuses on **Machine Learning**, with specific interests in **Reinforcement Learning (RL)**, **Manifold Learning**, and **Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs)**. I am deeply passionate about understanding how complex AI behaviors can be modeled and optimized.

## Research & Projects
I am currently exploring **Multi-Agent Systems**. One of my core research projects investigates how emergent turn-taking behaviors develop among independent agents operating in resource-constrained environments (for example, scenarios where multiple agents must share a single charging station). 

## Technical Skills
* **Programming & Frameworks:** Python, PyTorch
* **Tools & Methodologies:** Data Analysis, LaTeX

When I am not writing code or analyzing data, I enjoy exploring the intersection of technology and decision-making, as well as enjoying life in Okinawa with my family.
